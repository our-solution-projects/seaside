Holiday house in Kaltene

SeaSide Kaltene Holiday House is a family-created project that we have cherished for many years. The holiday house operates on the principle of "self-catering", providing a cozy rest. Everything we have created and established here, is done with warmth of heart, paying close attention to detail, rethinking the smallest details in every corner of the house. A cottage with style and fantastic surroundings, located 1.5 hours drive from Riga - in Kaltene, on the very edge of the sea. Here you will be able to enjoy the sea coast with the charm of rural romanticism - with many nice details, a firewood sauna, champagne on the terrace overlooking the sea, barbecue and SUP-ing in the sea.

We look forward to seeing you!

1st floor - lounge with kitchen (30.5m2)

Lounge where you can relax after sunbathing and barefoot enjoy the power of the oak floor.

1st floor - kitchen

Kitchen for preparing delicious home-cooked meals. A refrigerator with freezer, electric stove and electric kettle as well as pans, pots, bowls, plates, glasses, cutlery and kitchen towels are provided.

2nd floor - TV room (15.3m2)

Those who are tired of the sun also need a break. It can be done here in the TV room, in slippers and with a remote control in hand.
*children's bed (guest bed)

2nd floor - bedroom (14.7m2)

Double bed - blankets, pillows, bed linen, plaids.

Shared shower and WC - towels, shampoo, soap, toilet paper.
